miniwallism

by David Pierce (@da_pierce)
https://davidaaronpierce.com

For Ludum Dare 26 (4/28/2013)

You are a square dodging walls. If you get hit, you lose a chunk of yourself, but if you can hit the side of a wall as it comes you will add it to yourself and get bigger. Your score will only increase if you take risks and hit or get hit by walls.

Made with help from Foundation Game Design with HTML5 and JavaScript by Rex van der Spuy.
